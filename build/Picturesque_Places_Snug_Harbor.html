Looking for a cool place to take photographs? Well, Snug Harbor in Staten Island might be the place for you!

Snug Harbor contains a collection of 19th century buildings in an 83-acre park. The stunning, serene grounds are perfect for photography on a nice day, and while you're there don't forget to visit the botanical gardens!
